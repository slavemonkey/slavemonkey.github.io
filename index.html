The R & A Museum

Welcome to the R & A Museum. This exhibition explores four different acts of genocide and the implications of their respective international interventions.

As the most powerful actors in genocide situations, the international community as a collective is somewhat responsible for preventing these events from escalating or even occurring. Debating these things is pivotal in creating better responses to genocide and potential genocide in the future, so that less suffering will happen.
